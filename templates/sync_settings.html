Setup Instructions
Google Calendar
  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click "+" next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Paste your feed URL
  5. Click "Add calendar"
Outlook
  1. Go to Calendar view
  2. Click "Add calendar"
  3. Select "Subscribe from web"
  4. Paste your feed URL
  5. Click "Import"
